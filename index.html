Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. Student at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, the University of Hong Kong (HKU), supervised by Prof. Ngai WONG and Prof. Bei YU. I received M.S. in Computing Technology from ICT.CAS and B.S. in Computer Science and Technology from BISTU.
My research interests include HPC especially in parallel computing and deep learning compiler. I am also interested in distributed systems and applied artificial intelligence.
Before starting my PhD student career, I was a senior software engineer at Baidu. I was dedicated to the design and development of sharding management system and succinct data structure. I used to work on computer vision during my masters.
